From call centre to couture

Lee sat in a Sandton call centre, taking calls, dreaming of architecture. When she resigned, nothing made sense. Months of struggle, then a friend handed her R3000 — "start something". That side hustle became Lee Luxe. Today, two stores, a thousand stories, and a passion-driven mindset that refuses to quit.

This is not just a shoe brand. It's a movement for every woman who dares to walk taller.
